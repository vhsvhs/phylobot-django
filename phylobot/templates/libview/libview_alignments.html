Alignment Method

Alignment Method

Each alignment method applies a unique approach to determine which sequence sites are homologous and the placement of gaps.

To learn more about a particular alignment method, click the link [ref] next to its name.

 		N Taxa

N Taxa

The number of sequences aligned.

 		N Sites

N Sites

The number of sequence sites produced by this alignment method, including gaps. The number of sites in the alignment varies depending on the alignment method.

 		ZORRO Profile

ZORRO Profile

The ZORRO algorithm assigns a confidence score to each site in the alignment. Use the profile of ZORRO scores to determine the relative robustness of different regions of the alignment.

Reference:

Wu, Chatterji, and Eisen
Accounting For Alignment Uncertainty in Phylogenomics
PLoS One, January 17, 2012
DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0030288
